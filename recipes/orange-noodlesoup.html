Orange Noodle Soup recipe

The orange noodle soup is a delicious meal, originating from the ancient greek-japanese era.
It has been eaten and experienced by many people in the southern Europe and even in the central America
for being such a delicasy!

How to make it?

Firstly we will be needing some ingredients which can be bought from the local market:

ingredients

  • Oranges
  • Noodles
  • Hot water
  • Spices of your own choice

Step-By-Step food making

  1. Place noodles in a bowl
  2. Pour boiling water over noodles and let them sit
  3. Add spices of your own choice
  4. Peel orange while noodles are in water
  5. After 5 mins, pour out water and put in oranges
  6. Enjoy a delicious meal!
